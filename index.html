Welcome

The Vineyard's Storehouse provides top quality meat, farm fresh produce and other foods, and products at wholesale prices without the need for coupons, reward cards or discount cards.

The Owner

The Vineyard has opened the Commonwealth of Virginia’s first Non-profit grocery store with the emphasis on low prices for fresh nutrient rich foods. The Storehouse is owned by Jacob S. Hopson, a disabled American Veteran who courageously served with our U.S. Air Forces. We have a detailed plan to operate grocery stores where the consumer’s budget is paramount and having access to farm fresh produce and freshly butchered meat without a lot of harmful additives.
